The Kasthuri Medical Mission

Welcome to Kasthuri Medical Mission, A Public Health mission to promote Medical education, Health science and to Educate in
regular education system for medical students.where we are dedicated to transforming medical education and empowering aspiring
medical professionals. Join us in our mission to learn high-quality medical education which is accessible to all.
